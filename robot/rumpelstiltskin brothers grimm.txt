'' when the girl be alone the manikin come again for the third time , and say , `` what will you give I if I spin the straw for you this time also ? '' then the Queen begin to weep and cry , so that the manikin pity she . `` I will give you three day ' time , '' say he , `` if by that time you find out my name , then shall you keep you child . '' on the second day she have inquiry make in the neighborhood as to the name of the people there , and she repeat to the manikin the most uncommon and curious . `` perhaps you name be Shortribs , or Sheepshanks , or Laceleg ?
