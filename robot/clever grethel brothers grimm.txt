77 Clever Grethel BrothersS Grimm there be once a cook name Grethel , who wear shoe with red rosette , and when she walk out with they on , she turn herself this way and that , and think , `` you certainly be a pretty girl ! '' and when she come home she drink , in she gladness of heart , a draught of wine , and as wine excite a desire to eat , she taste the best of whatever she be cook until she be satisfy , and say , `` the cook must know what the food be like . '' it come to pass that the master one day say to she , `` Grethel , there be a guest come this evening ; prepare I two fowl very daintily . '' `` I will see to it , master , '' answer Grethel . she kill two fowl , scald they , pluck they , put they on the spit , and towards evening set they before the fire , that they might roast . the fowl begin to turn brown , and be nearly ready , but the guest have not yet arrive . then Grethel call out to she master , `` if the guest do not come , I must take the fowl away from the fire , but it will be a sin and a shame if they be not eat directly , when they be juiciest . '' the master say , `` I will run myself , and fetch the guest . '' when the master have turn he back , Grethel lay the spit with the fowl on one side , and think , `` stand so long by the fire there , make one hot and thirsty ; who know when they will come ? meanwhile , I will run into the cellar , and take a drink . '' she run down , set a jug , say , `` God bless it to thy use , Grethel , '' and take a good drink , and take yet another hearty draught . then she go and put the fowl down again to the fire , baste they , and drive the spit merrily round . but as the roast meat smell so good , Grethel think , `` something might be wrong , it ought to be taste ! '' she touch it with she finger , and say , `` ah ! how good fowl be ! it certainly be a sin and a shame that they be not eat directly ! '' she run to the window , to see if the master be not come with he guest , but she see no one , and go back to the fowl and thought , `` one of the wing be burn ! I have better take it off and eat it . '' so she cut it off , eat it , and enjoy it , and when she have do , she think , `` the other must go down too , or else master will observe that something be miss . '' when the two wing be eat , she go and look for she master , and do not see he . it suddenly occur to she , `` who know ? they be perhaps not come at all , and have turn in somewhere . '' then she say , `` Hallo , Grethel , enjoy yourself , one fowl have be cut into , take another drink , and eat it up entirely ; when it be eat you will have some peace , why should God 's good gift be spoil ? '' so she run into the cellar again , take a enormous drink and eat up the one chicken in great glee . when one of the chicken be swallow down , and still she master do not come , Grethel look at the other and say , `` where one be , the other should be likewise , the two go together ; what be right for the one be right for the other ; I think if I be to take another draught it would do I no harm . '' so she take another hearty drink , and let the second chicken rejoin the first . while she be just in the best of the eating , she master come and cry , hurry up , `` haste thee , Grethel , the guest be come directly after I ! '' `` yes , sir , I will soon serve up , '' answer Grethel . meantime the master look to see that the table be properly lay , and take the great knife , wherewith he be go to carve the chicken , and sharpen it on the step . presently the guest come , and knock politely and courteously at the house-door . Grethel run , and look to see who be there , and when she see the guest , she put she finger to she lip and say , `` hush ! hush ! get away as quickly as you can , if my master catch you it will be the worse for you ; he certainly do ask you to supper , but he intention be to cut off you two ear . just listen how he be sharpen the knife for it ! '' the guest hear the sharpen , and hurry down the step again as fast as he could . Grethel be not idle ; she run scream to she master , and cry , `` you have invite a fine guest ! '' `` Eh , why , Grethel ? what do you mean by that ? '' `` yes , '' say she , `` he have take the chicken which I be just go to serve up , off the dish , and have run away with they ! '' `` that be a nice trick ! '' say she master , and lament the fine chicken . `` if he have but leave I one , so that something remain for I to eat . '' he call to he to stop , but the guest pretend not to hear . then he run after he with the knife still in he hand , cry , `` just one , just one , '' mean that the guest should leave he just one chicken , and not take both . the guest , however , think no otherwise than that he be to give up one of he ear , and run as if fire be burn under he , in order to take they both home with he .
