162 the Wise Servant BrothersS Grimm how fortunate be the master , and how well all go in he house , when he have a wise servant who listen to he order and do not obey they , but prefer follow he own wisdom . a clever John of this kind be once send out by he master to seek a lose cow . he stay away a long time , and the master thought , `` Faithful John do not spare any pain over he work ! '' as , however , he do not come back at all , the master be afraid lest some misfortune have befall he , and set out himself to look for he . he have to search a long time , but at last he perceive the boy who be run up and down a large field . `` now , dear John , '' say the master when he have get up to he , `` hast thou find the cow which I send thee to seek ? '' `` no , master , '' he answer , `` I have not find the cow , but then I have not look for it . '' `` then what hast thou look for , John ? '' `` something better , and that luckily I have find . '' `` what be that , John ? '' `` three blackbird , '' answer the boy . `` and where be they ? '' ask the master . `` I see one of they , I hear the other , and I be run after the third , '' answer the wise boy . take example by this , do not trouble yourselve about you master or they order , but rather do what come into you head and please you , and then you will act just as wisely as prudent John .
