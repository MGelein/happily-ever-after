117 the Wilful Child BrothersS Grimm once upon a time there be a child who be willful , and would not do at she mother wish . for this reason God have no pleasure in she , and let she become ill , and no doctor could do she any good , and in a short time she lay on she death-bed . when she have be lower into she grave , and the earth be spread over she , all at once she arm come out again , and stretch upward , and when they have put it in and spread fresh earth over it , it be all to no purpose , for the arm always come out again . then the mother herself be oblige to go to the grave , and strike the arm with a rod , and when she have do that , it be draw in , and then at last the child have rest beneath the ground .
