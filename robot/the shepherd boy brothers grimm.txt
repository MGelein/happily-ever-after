152 the Shepherd Boy BrothersS Grimm there be once on a time a shepherd boy whose fame spread far and wide because of the wise answer which he give to every question . the King of the country hear of it likewise , but do not believe it , and send for the boy . then he say to he , `` if thou canst give I a answer to three question which I will ask thee , I will look on thee as my own child , and thou shalt dwell with I in my royal palace . '' the boy say , `` what be the three question ? '' the King say , `` the first be , how many drop of water be there in the ocean ? '' the shepherd boy answer , `` Lord King , if you will have all the river on earth dam up so that not a single drop run from they into the sea until I have count it , I will tell you how many drop there be in the sea . '' the King say , `` the next question be , how many star be there in the sky ? '' the shepherd boy say , `` give I a great sheet of white paper , '' and then he make so many fine point on it with a pen that they could scarcely be see , and it be all but impossible to count they ; any one who look at they would have lose he sight . then he say , `` there be as many star in the sky as there be point on the paper ; just count they . '' but no one be able to do it . the King say , `` the third question be , how many seconds of time be there in eternity . '' then say the shepherd boy , `` in Lower Pomerania be the Diamond Mountain , which be two mile and a half high , two mile and a half wide , and two mile and a half in depth ; every hundred year a little bird come and sharpen its beak on it , and when the whole mountain be worn away by this , then the first second of eternity will be over . '' the King say , `` Thou hast answer the three question like a wise man , and shalt henceforth dwell with I in my royal palace , and I will regard thee as my own child . ''
