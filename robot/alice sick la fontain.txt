ALICE SICK La Fontain SICK , Alice grow , and fear dire event , some friend advise a servant should be send she confessor to bring and ease she mind ; -- Yes , she reply , to see he I be inclined ; let father Andrew instantly be seek : -- by he salvation usually I be teach . a messenger be tell , without delay , to take , with rapid step , the convent way ; he ring the bell -- a monk enquire he name , and ask for what , or whom , the fellow come . I father Andrew want , the wight reply , who be oft to Alice confessor and guide : with Andrew , cry the other , would you speak ? if that be the case , he be far enough to seek ; Poor man ! he be leave we for the region bless , and have in Paradise ten year confess .
