145 the Ungrateful Son BrothersS Grimm by BrothersS Grimm a man and he wife be once sit by the door of they house , and they have a roasted chicken set before they , and be about to eat it together . then the man see that he age father be come , and hastily take the chicken and hide it , for he would not permit he to have any of it . the old man come , take a drink , and go away . now the son want to put the roasted chicken on the table again , but when he take it up , it have become a great toad , which jump into he face and sit there and never go away again , and if any one want to take it off , it look venomously at he as if it would jump in he face , so that no one would venture to touch it . and the ungrateful son be force to feed the toad every day , or else it feed itself on he face ; and thus he go about the world without know rest .
