125 the Devil and he Grandmother BrothersS Grimm there be a great war , and the King have many soldier , but give they small pay , so small that they could not live upon it , so three of they agree among themselves to desert . one of they say to the other , `` if we be catch we shall be hang on the gallows ; how shall we manage it ? '' another say , `` look at that great cornfield , if we be to hide ourselves there , no one could find we ; the troops be not allow to enter it , and to-morrow they be to march away . '' they creep into the corn , only the troops do not march away , but remain lie all round about it . they stay in the corn for two day and two night , and be so hungry that they all but die , but if they have come out , they death would have be certain . then say they , `` what be the use of we desert if we have to perish miserably here ? '' but now a fiery dragon come fly through the air , and it come down to they , and ask why they have conceal themselves there ? they answer , `` we be three soldier who have desert because the pay be so bad , and now we shall have to die of hunger if we stay here , or to dangle on the gallows if we go out . '' `` if you will serve I for seven year , '' say the dragon , `` I will convey you through the army so that no one shall seize you . '' `` we have no choice and be compel to accept , '' they reply . then the dragon catch hold of they with he claw , and carry they away through the air over the army , and put they down again on the earth far from it ; but the dragon be no other than the Devil . he give they a small whip and say , `` Whip with it and crack it , and then as much gold will spring up round about as you can wish for ; then you can live like great lord , keep horse , and drive you carriage , but when the seven year have come to a end , you be my property . '' then he put before they a book which they be all three force to sign . `` I will , however , then set you a riddle , '' say he , `` and if you can guess that , you shall be free , and release from my power . '' then the dragon fly away from they , and they go away with they whip , have gold in plenty , order themselves rich apparel , and travel about the world . wherever they be they live in pleasure and magnificence , ride on horseback , drive in carriage , eat and drank , but do nothing wicked . the time slip quickly away , and when the seven year be come to a end , two of they be terribly anxious and alarmed ; but the third take the affair easily , and say , `` Brothers , fear nothing , my head be sharp enough , I shall guess the riddle . '' they go out into the open country and sit down , and the two pull sorrowful face . then a age woman come up to they who inquire why they be so sad ? `` Alas ! '' say they , `` how can that concern you ? after all , you can not help we . '' `` who know ? '' say she . `` confide you trouble to I . '' so they tell she that they have be the Devil 's servant for nearly seven year , and that he have provide they with gold as plentifully as if it have be blackberry , but that they have sell themselves to he , and be forfeit to he , if at the end of the seven year they could not guess a riddle . the old woman say , `` if you be to be save , one of you must go into the forest , there he will come to a fallen rock which look like a little house , he must enter that , and then he will obtain help . '' the two melancholy one think to themselves , `` that will still not save we , '' and stay where they be , but the third , the merry one , get up and walk on in the forest until he find the rock-house . in the little house , however , a very age woman be sit , who be the Devil 's grandmother , and ask the soldier where he come from , and what he want there ? he tell she everything that have happen , and as he please she well , she have pity on he , and say she would help he . she lift up a great stone which lay above a cellar , and say , `` conceal thyself there , thou canst hear everything that be say here ; only sit still , and do not stir . when the dragon come , I will question he about the riddle , he tell everything to I , so listen carefully to he answer . '' at twelve o'clock at night , the dragon come fly thither , and ask for he dinner . the grandmother lay the table , and serve up food and drink , so that he be pleased , and they eat and drink together . in the course of conversation , she ask he what kind of a day he have have , and how many soul he have get ? `` nothing go very well to-day , '' he answer , `` but I have lay hold of three soldier , I have they safe . '' `` indeed ! three soldier , that be something like , but they may escape you yet . '' the Devil say mockingly , `` they be mine ! I will set they a riddle , which they will never in this world be able to guess ! '' `` what riddle be that ? '' she inquire . `` I will tell you . in the great North Sea lie a dead dog-fish , that shall be you roast meat , and the rib of a whale shall be you silver spoon , and a hollow old horse 's hoof shall be you wine-glass . '' when the Devil have go to bed , the old grandmother raise up the stone , and let out the soldier . `` Hast thou pay particular attention to everything ? '' `` yes , '' say he , `` I know enough , and will contrive to save myself . '' then he have to go back another way , through the window , secretly and with all speed to he companion . he tell they how the Devil have be overreach by the old grandmother , and how he have learn the answer to the riddle from he . then they be all joyous , and of good cheer , and take the whip and whip so much gold for themselves that it run all over the ground . when the seven year have fully go by , the Devil come with the book , show the signature , and say , `` I will take you with I to hell . there you shall have a meal ! if you can guess what kind of roast meat you will have to eat , you shall be free and release from you bargain , and may keep the whip as well . '' then the first soldier begin and say , `` in the great North Sea lie a dead dog-fish , that no doubt be the roast meat . '' the Devil be angry , and begin to mutter , `` hm ! hm ! hm ! '' and ask the second , `` but what will you spoon be ? '' `` the rib of a whale , that be to be we silver spoon . '' the Devil make a wry face , again growl , `` hm ! hm ! hm ! '' and say to the third , `` and do you also know what you wine-glass be to be ? '' `` a old horse 's hoof be to be we wineglass . '' then the Devil fly away with a loud cry , and have no more power over they , but the three keep the whip , whip as much money for themselves with it as they want , and live happily to they end .
