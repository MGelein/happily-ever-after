160 a Riddling Tale BrothersS Grimm three woman be change into flower which grow in the field , but one of they be allow to be in she own home at night . then once when day be draw near , and she be force to go back to she companion in the field and become a flower again , she say to she husband , `` if thou wilt come this afternoon and gather I , I shall be set free and henceforth stay with thee . '' and he do so . now the question be , how do she husband know she , for the flower be exactly alike , and without any difference ? answer : as she be at she home during the night and not in the field , no dew fall on she as it do on the other , and by this she husband know she .
