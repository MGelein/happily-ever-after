75 the Fox and the Cat BrothersS Grimm by BrothersS Grimm it happen that the cat meet the fox in a forest , and as she think to herself , `` he be clever and full of experience , and much esteem in the world , '' she speak to he in a friendly way . `` good-day , dear Mr. Fox , how be you ? how be all with you ? how be you get through this dear season ? '' the fox , full of all kind of arrogance , look at the cat from head to foot , and for a long time do not know whether he would give any answer or not . at last he say , `` oh , thou wretched beard-cleaner , thou piebald fool , thou hungry mouse-hunter , what canst thou be think of ? Dost thou venture to ask how I be get on ? what hast thou learn ? how many art dost thou understand ? '' `` I understand but one , '' reply the cat , modestly . `` what art be that ? '' ask the fox . `` when the hound be follow I , I can spring into a tree and save myself . '' `` be that all ? '' say the fox . `` I be master of a hundred art , and have into the bargain a sackful of cunning . Thou makest I sorry for thee ; come with I , I will teach thee how people get away from the hound . '' just then come a hunter with four dog . the cat spring nimbly up a tree , and sit down on top of it , where the branch and foliage quite conceal she . `` open you sack , Mr. Fox , open you sack , '' cry the cat to he , but the dog have already seize he , and be hold he fast . `` ah , Mr. Fox , '' cry the cat . `` you with you hundred art be leave in the lurch ! have you be able to climb like I , you would not have lose you life .
